Fandong Meng (孟凡东)

Personal Information

He is a principal researcher and team leader at Pattern Recognition Center, WeChat, Tencent.

He got his Ph.D. degree at Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences.

His research focuses on deep learning for machine translation and natural language processing.
